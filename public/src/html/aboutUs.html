Melissa Paauwe

UESCA Certified Running Coach
UESCA Certified Ultrarunning Coach

Melissa dabbled in running her whole life but it wasn't until she enlisted the help of a Coach did she really see her potential. With progress came confidence, in all aspects of her life, encouraging to her start her own coaching business to help others find their potential. Melissa's approach to her clients is very individualized - everyone's plans are designed for them and all levels are welcome in the WRTWC community.

Melissa has raced at elite levels in the full and half marathon, with top 10 finishes nationally in both events. She won the Alberta Provincial Time Trial in road cycling in 2017, and has represented Canada at Multisport World Championships (Elite Duathlon, after claiming the national title in 2017). Her greatest accomplishment to date was running the Boston Marathon and finishing 2nd Canadian female.

Laura Henderson

UESCA Certified Coach

Laura is a CPA who is happily married with 2 young children and a big fur baby. Laura loves to spend time with her family and being outdoors as much as possible. She is also passionate about travel and playing/watching sports of almost all varieties.

Laura's love of running started when she ran her first half marathon in 2011 and it has only grown from there. She has now run 8 marathons, 40+ half marathons, and 50+ shorter distance races. The highlight of Laura’s running career so far has been fulfilling her long-time dream of qualifying for and running the Boston Marathon, which she accomplished in 2023! She is now halfway in her journey to complete the 6 World Major marathons.

Laura joined WRTWC as a coach so that she can share her joy of running and help others to see what they’re capable of. She wants people to see that anyone can be a runner, you just need the right plan, motivation, and community to support you!

Leslie Jones

UESCA Certified Coach
